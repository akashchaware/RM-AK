How It Works

Three secure, transparent steps designed to give your smartphone premium service without leaving your room.

📦
Step 1

Free Doorstep Pickup

Schedule a collection slot. Our verified logistic personnel collects your locked device securely using dynamic 6-digit handover codes.

🔬
Step 2

Laboratory Repair

Technicians calibrate diagnostics under anti-static setups. Private data files remain isolated in secure sandbox partitions.

🚀
Step 3

Doorstep Delivery

Get your phone delivered back. Perform on-the-spot touch/calibration checks, then complete safe escrow transaction payouts.

Maintenance Mode Active Guarantee

To protect your private files, chats, bank logins, and media, our technicians enforce strict Maintenance Sandbox Mode locks on active pickups. No passwords needed. For Dead Phones or Blank Screens, isolation is verified physically in the high-security diagnostics cage.
